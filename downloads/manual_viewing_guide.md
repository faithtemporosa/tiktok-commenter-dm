## Daily Routine (1 Hour Total)

### **Morning Session (20 mins) - Account 1**

**Time:** 9:00 AM (or whenever you have coffee)

1. Open TikTok on iPhone
2. Switch to **Account 1**
3. For each target account:

**Target 1: @charlidamelio (7 mins)**
- Tap Search → Type "charlidamelio"
- Tap on @charlidamelio profile
- Tap first video
- Watch 15-20 seconds
- Swipe up
- Repeat 10 times (10 videos)

**Target 2: @addisonre (7 mins)**
- Tap Search → Type "addisonre"
- Same process → 10 videos

**Target 3: @bellapoarch (7 mins)**
- Same process → 10 videos

**Total:** 30 videos, 20 minutes

---

### **Afternoon Session (20 mins) - Account 2**

**Time:** 2:00 PM (or during lunch break)

- Same process as morning
- Switch to **Account 2** first
- View same 3 target accounts
- 30 more videos

---

### **Evening Session (20 mins) - Account 3**

## Macro/Shortcut for Faster Switching

At least automate the TikTok account switching:

### Create iOS Shortcut for Account Switching

1. Open **Shortcuts** app on iPhone
2. Create shortcut: **"Switch TikTok Account"**
3. Actions:
   - Open TikTok
   - Wait 2 seconds
   - *You manually tap Profile → Username → Select account*

This saves a few taps but you still control everything.
