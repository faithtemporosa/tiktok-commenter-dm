## Step-by-Step: Automatic Tap Setup

### **1. Install on Android Device/Emulator**

1. Open **Google Play Store**
2. Search: **"Automatic Tap"**
3. Install: **"Automatic Tap - Auto Clicker"** by SimpleHat Software
4. Open the app

### **2. Grant Permissions**

1. App will ask for **Accessibility** permission
2. Go to Settings → Accessibility → Automatic Tap
3. Toggle **ON**
4. Go back to Automatic Tap app

### **3. Open TikTok**

1. Open TikTok app
2. Login to your account
3. Navigate to a video (any video)
4. Keep TikTok open

### **4. Record Swipe Gesture**

1. Open **Automatic Tap** (floating button appears)
2. Tap the floating button → **"Record"**
3. In TikTok:
   - **Swipe UP** (from bottom to top of screen)
   - **Wait 15 seconds** (just wait, app is recording)
   - **Swipe UP** again
   - **Wait 15 seconds**
   - **Swipe UP** again
   - **Wait 15 seconds**
4. Tap Automatic Tap button → **"Stop"**

### **5. Configure Replay**

1. In Automatic Tap settings:
   - **Loop count:** 10 (this will repeat your 3-swipe sequence 10 times = 30 videos)
   - **Speed:** 1x (normal speed)
   - **Delay before start:** 3 seconds

### **6. Run Automation**

1. Open TikTok, go to first video
2. Tap Automatic Tap button → **"Play"**
3. **Put device down**
4. Automation runs for ~20 minutes
5. Watches ~30 videos automatically!

### **7. Repeat for Other Accounts**

1. Switch TikTok account
2. Tap Automatic Tap → "Play"
3. Done!
